My logo is designed to convey several ideas. The three lines, which progressively shorten, form an “F” to represent my work as a freelancer. The filter-inspired shape reflects my focus on elevating raw data through careful cleaning and refinement. In addition, the three lines symbolize the core stages of ETL: Extract, Transform, and Load. Viewed from the side, the mark can also be interpreted as a bar chart, reinforcing the theme of data and analytics. Finally, the gold color reflects the premium, high-quality standard I bring to every project.
